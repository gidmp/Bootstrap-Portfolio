About Me

Test test 1, 2, 3...Hi, My name is Daniel Luke Tanoeihusada. I am a Lab Assistant that manages Histology quality control in University of Washington Medical center, arguably one of the largest hospital in America. On the side, I am a coding bootcamp student in University of Washington. I was born in Los Angeles and raised in America and sent back to the states in a quest that everyone is in for. Building a good life.

My hobbies used to be playing video games and watching youtube at home; extremely enjoyable but also a very unproductive one. Realizing that it will not pay any dividend, I decided to get out of my comfort zone and try to learn coding, which I found surprisingly enjoyable. Sure, it is not the easiest thing to learn, but learning how to build a webpage and solving tough puzzle-like problems are really fascinating.

I think being lost in life and having no sense of purpose is a very frustrating and demoralizing feeling, oh and I know it very well. That is why, I believe, having a goal to walk towards to is very important.
